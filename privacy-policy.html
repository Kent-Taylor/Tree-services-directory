Legal and privacy

Privacy policy

This policy explains the limited information involved in operating this static directory and the advertising technologies that may be used.

Effective: July 13, 2026 · Publisher: Kent Taylor · Contact: kredfox4@gmail.com

Information you provide

This site has no user account, checkout or onsite contact form. If you email the publisher, your email address, message and any attachments are received through the publisher's email provider and used to respond, investigate corrections, prevent abuse and maintain necessary records. Do not send sensitive personal information.

Hosting and technical logs

The site is hosted through GitHub Pages. As with typical web hosting, GitHub and network providers may process request data such as IP address, browser or device type, requested URL, timestamp and diagnostic or security information. The publisher does not receive a named visitor list from the static site. Review GitHub's privacy statement (opens in a new tab) for its practices.

Google advertising and cookies

This site uses or intends to use Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on a visitor's prior visits to this or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on visits to this site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising through Google My Ad Center (opens in a new tab). You may also review broader industry choices at WebChoices (opens in a new tab). Google explains how it uses information from partner sites in How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services (opens in a new tab).

Consent choices

Where applicable law requires it, a consent message may be displayed before non-essential advertising technologies are used. Choices offered through that message can be revisited through the controls Google provides. Browser settings may also block or delete cookies, although this can affect site or ad functionality.

Third-party links

Directory listings and sources link to provider, government, university and other third-party sites. Their privacy practices are controlled by their respective operators. Following an external link is your choice.

Children, retention and requests

This general-audience directory is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal information. Correction emails are retained only as long as reasonably needed to resolve the request, document the change, meet legal obligations or prevent abuse. To ask about a message you sent or request deletion where applicable, contact the publisher.

Policy changes

Material updates will be reflected by changing the effective date on this page. Continued site use after an update is subject to the revised policy.
