Trust and transparency

Editorial and ranking methodology

These rules govern every directory listing and explain what a verification date does—and does not—mean.

Authoritative sources

The provider's own website is the required source for a directory listing. We do not use Google Business profiles, review platforms, data brokers, social-profile aggregators or lead lists as the factual basis for a listing. Government and university sources are used for public guidance articles, not to evaluate companies.

Inclusion rules

A listing is eligible when an official provider website establishes both (1) tree-related work and (2) service in Knoxville or an explicitly stated nearby Knoxville-area community. Forestry mulching or land clearing may appear as its own category when the provider connects that work to Knoxville. Inclusion is free.

What is excluded

  • Businesses without a working, identifiable official website.
  • Providers whose own source does not establish the relevant service or area.
  • Unrelated lawn, construction or utility businesses without a homeowner-relevant tree service.
  • Copied photos, reviews, ratings, review counts, owner posts, popularity data, third-party emails or employee details.
  • Claims about price, quality, availability, licensing, insurance or credentials that cannot be responsibly represented from the authoritative source.

Writing and verification

Listing summaries are original, neutral descriptions of the services and coverage stated on the linked website. A verification date records the last manual source check. It does not guarantee that a provider is open, available, qualified for a specific hazard or unchanged after that date. Readers must confirm current details.

Emergency labels

“Emergency service advertised” appears only when the provider's website explicitly promotes emergency tree work or emergency availability. The label does not promise round-the-clock staffing, a response time or current availability.

Ordering and advertising

The default order is alphabetical. “Recently verified” is an optional date sort. No quality score is calculated, no business is designated “best” or “cheapest,” and no provider can pay to change its order. Advertising may support this publication but is separated from listing inclusion and editorial decisions.

Review cycle and corrections

The complete dataset is scheduled for review at least twice each year. Reports of broken links, changed services or incorrect descriptions are checked against authoritative evidence between review cycles. See Corrections and listing claims to submit a report.

Methodology effective and last reviewed July 13, 2026.
